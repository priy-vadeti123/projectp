What is Network Security?

Network security refers to the measures taken to protect data and networks from unauthorized access, misuse, malfunction, modification, or destruction. It ensures the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of information.

Types of Network Security

  • Firewall Protection
  • Intrusion Detection Systems
  • Virtual Private Networks (VPN)
  • Encryption
  • Antivirus and Antimalware

Why is Network Security Important?

With increasing cyber threats, securing networks has become essential to protect personal, business, and government data. Network security helps prevent data breaches, identity theft, and financial loss.
